## CRITICAL CONSTRAINTS

### MUST
- Display all three weather indicators (wind/rain/temp) in a compact horizontal strip
- Highlight the worst condition across all overlays (e.g., heavy rain takes priority)
- Be reusable across route option cards and route detail views
- Tap on strip to toggle between showing single worst vs all conditions

### NEVER
- Duplicate badge components - compose existing badges
- Show more than one 'worst' indicator at a time in compact mode

### STRICTLY
- Strip must fit within card width constraints
- Use semantic theme colors consistently

## SPECIFICATION

**Objective:** Create a compact weather-strip component that summarizes all weather conditions with the worst condition visually highlighted.

**Success looks like:** Route comparison view shows a weather strip per route that immediately communicates the most concerning weather condition while allowing expansion to see all conditions.
