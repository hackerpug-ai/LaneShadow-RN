- Persist until SaveFavoriteSheet renders

**Haptic Feedback**:
- Use expo-haptics or React Native Haptics
- Style: Light or Medium impact
- Trigger: Once on long-press completion
- Fallback: No haptics if platform doesn't support

**Gesture Conflicts**:
- Long-press shouldn't trigger on map pan
- Long-press shouldn't interfere with zoom pinch
- Short tap (<500ms) should select route (existing behavior)
- Consider gesture priority: long-press > tap > pan

**Testing**:

**Platform Considerations**:

**Performance**:

**Edge Cases**:
