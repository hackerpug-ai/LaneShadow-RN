§01

Purpose & Atomic Composition

LSToolbar is the universal top navigation bar composing LSButton (UC-ATM-02) icon variants into leading and trailing slots, with a centered title rendered at typography.ui.title.md. Height is pinned to sizing.component.toolbarHeight (56px) — no organism may stretch or shrink it.

LSNavHeader extends the concept with an optional large-title variant that renders the title in typography.opinion.lg (Newsreader 22px) when scrolled to top, collapsing to typography.ui.title.md on scroll. Both molecules consume surface and content tokens — never raw colors. Both respect platform safe areas: iOS Dynamic Island / notch adds 48pt above the toolbar; Android WindowInsets adds 24pt.

§02c

Dark Mode — Inverted Palette

Dark mode is a token swap, never a new component. Scoping .mode-dark on any ancestor flips color.surface.* to the warm ink ladder (ink-800 primary, ink-700 card, ink-600 inset), lifts content to ink-050 / ink-100 / ink-200, and softens borders to translucent paper (rgba(242,238,232,0.12)). Copper signal holds — it is the brand constant across both themes.

LSToolbar and LSNavHeader re-render identically: same 56px height, same typography scale, same icon-button geometry. Only elev.chrome and action.ghost shift to dark-appropriate values. The collapsed NavHeader's scroll shadow deepens to 0 1px 3px rgba(0,0,0,0.24) + 0 4px 14px rgba(0,0,0,0.28) to remain legible against the warm ink surface.

§05

Motion Recipes

navHeaderCollapse — when scroll offset exceeds the large-title height, the title cross-fades from typography.opinion.lg to typography.ui.title.md. Transition: 200ms ease-in-out on opacity and a simultaneous 4px translateY. The toolbar shadow appears at scroll offset 1px: box-shadow transitions from 0 to elev.chrome in 150ms.

toolbarEnter — on screen push the toolbar does not animate independently. The entire screen slides in via the navigator transition recipe (spring 400ms, damping 32). Icon tap: background flashes to action.ghost for 120ms, then releases.

§06

Rationale + AC Traceability

The 56px toolbar height is the only value in the system that is not derived from a spacing step. It was chosen empirically to match iOS HIG's 44pt navigation bar + 12pt comfortable breathing room, landing on a value that feels cohesive alongside a 44pt touch-target icon button. Locking it as sizing.component.toolbarHeight prevents screen-level code from ever rendering a 48px or 60px variant by accident.

The large-title variant uses Newsreader (opinion typeface) rather than Geist because large headers are editorial moments — LaneShadow is a lifestyle product and the serif drop-in reinforces brand voice at section entry points.
