§01 · Purpose

A map that listens — not a form that asks.

IdleScreen is the first thing a rider sees when the Navigator is dormant. There is no feed, no profile, no "tap to continue." The entire surface is a warm paper topographic map with a single Newsreader-serif opinion floating above it: where are we riding today? Every subsequent screen is a refinement of that question.

The idle state has to accomplish three things without feeling crowded: it must orient the rider (what day, what weather, what region), invite conversation through a chat input with four primed suggestion chips, and reveal nearby favorites as copper pins without overwhelming the map. The Newsreader italicization of today is the one rhetorical flourish — a printed-page voice that signals the Navigator is authored, not generated.

No polyline is drawn yet. No Navigator message is pinned. The rider owns the cursor. Tapping a suggestion, typing, or opening the filter sheet transitions the screen into PlanningScreen (UC-SCR-02).

§06 · Rationale

Why this question, this silence.

Design principles honored

The Idle screen is the product's tone-setter. Every choice — the warm-paper ground, the Newsreader italic on today, the four suggestion chips (not eight, not two) — exists to establish that the Navigator listens rather than interrogates. A feed-style home would've worked. It would've also killed the character the product depends on.

The glass chrome (chat input, top-bar chips, tag pills) sits on top of the map without ever fully covering it. The rider can always read the landscape through the UI.

AC traceability

  • AC-01 S01 · Top bar + greeting overlay with italicized "today" + 4-chip LSChatInput + MANUAL tag.
  • AC-02 iOS + Android identical — mirrored in S01 & S03 light/dark pairing.
  • AC-03 Suggestion tap fires onSuggestionTap — demonstrated in S02.
  • AC-04 Typing swaps sliders → send — demonstrated in S02.
  • AC-05 Hamburger tap fires presentSessions stub — covered in §05 motion table.
  • AC-06 Light/dark re-renders tokens — demonstrated S01 ↔ S03.
  • AC-07 No data-fetching logic; IdleMockProvider injects greeting/location/suggestions.
