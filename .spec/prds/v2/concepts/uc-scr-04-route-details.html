§01 · Purpose

Numbers in mono, prose in serif, decision in copper.

RouteDetailsScreen is the commitment surface. The rider has picked a route; the map reduces to that single polyline framed with spacing.4 padding. Everything else is in the sheet: the best badge as the Navigator's seal of approval, the Newsreader title ("The Skyline Spine" — these are names, not paths), a four-column instrument readout in JetBrains Mono, six cells of per-hour weather, and a sticky action row.

The instrument readout is where the three typography families earn their keep. Opinion names the route. UI labels the fields ("DIST", "TIME"). Instrument carries the numbers ("64 mi", "2h 10m") — tabular-aligned, copper-tinted when the metric is a positive signal (scenic score), neutral otherwise. A rider reading mid-glance can scan mono columns faster than sans labels.

The weather timeline is the sheet's second opinion. Six hours from start to end, each cell tinted from the condition token (color.weather.*.tint). If the weather changes mid-ride, the sheet tells the rider where it changes, not just that it does.

§06 · Rationale

Why this much, and not more.

Design principles honored

A motorcycle rider making a commit decision doesn't want a wall of text — they want the five numbers that matter plus enough weather context to know whether the ride survives the afternoon. The instrument readout in JetBrains Mono gives the page its dashboard character without lapsing into a dashboard's visual noise. The opinion-serif route name keeps the Navigator's voice present even on the decision screen.

Save is outline. Ride this is primary. The 1:2 flex ratio isn't an accident — the primary action is visually heavier because it is the heavier action. Save is reversible; Ride is the handoff.

AC traceability

  • AC-01 Best badge, opinion title, 4-col instrument readout, 6-hr wx timeline, Save + Ride — S01.
  • AC-02 iOS + Android identical.
  • AC-03 Save and Ride callbacks fire; stub logs.
  • AC-04 Detent drag morphs sheet; dismiss threshold triggers onDismiss.
  • AC-05 Weather timeline renders per-condition tints; includes mixed-weather variant — S02.
