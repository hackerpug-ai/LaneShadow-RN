## UC consolidation decisions

- DATA group (convex-planner UC-DATA-01..06) carried over unchanged as the canonical DATA-01..06: DATA-01 geospatial seed, DATA-02 archetype mapping layer, DATA-03 curatedRouteRef field, DATA-04 state-normalize + length-clamp, DATA-05 listCuratedRoutes, DATA-06 getCuratedRouteDetail. No conflicts within DATA; it was the sole authority on the backend read path.
- MERGE: PM's UC-DISC-01 ('Discovery is the home and shows real nearby routes') and RN's UC-DISC-02 ('Mount Discovery as DEFAULT HOME and demote chat to a Plan-a-ride drawer entry') describe the same contract from the product lens (Discovery IS the default home) and the implementation lens (route-ordering/redirect + drawer rewiring). Merged into final UC-DISC-02 'Discovery is the default home and the chat planning agent is demoted to a Plan-a-ride drawer entry', combining PM's nearby-ranking ACs with RN's default-landing + drawer + unmodified-chat ACs.
- KEPT DISTINCT: PM's UC-DISC-00 (full discover-to-ride journey) became final UC-DISC-01 and is retained as the cross-cutting capstone the D9 gate verifies, NOT folded into any single feature UC, because it is the one end-to-end arc that stitches DATA+DISC+DTL+SAVE and is the true acceptance signal.
- KEPT DISTINCT: PM's UC-DISC-02 (rider-facing browse-by-state journey) became final UC-DISC-03. Distinct from the backend normalization (DATA-04) which it depends on, and distinct from the default-home UC, because it owns the rider-facing 'find a road in a state I'm curious about' half of the entry point.
- KEPT DISTINCT: RN's UC-DISC-01 (useCuratedDiscovery hook data-shape contract) became final UC-DISC-04. Retained as its own UC because the hook is the System-facing seam between the live query and the screen, with integration-test ACs (loading/empty conventions, 0-1 carry-through, center derivation) that are not covered by the screen-render UCs.
- KEPT DISTINCT: RN's UC-DISC-03 (wire RouteDiscoveryScreen from MOCK_ROUTES to live hook with chips/sort/overlays) became final UC-DISC-05. Distinct from the hook UC (DISC-04) and the legibility UC (DISC-07): this owns the mock->live replacement and the on-device render of real pins/chips/sort.
- KEPT DISTINCT: RN's UC-DISC-04 (standardize Discovery pins on MapboxMapView) became final UC-DISC-06. The map-engine convergence is a high-risk, separately-verifiable concern (pins must render and be tappable under Mapbox, coordinate-format trap) that warrants its own UC and gating check.
- MERGE-ADJACENT: Designer's UC-DISC-05 (discovery legibility: pin density, 44dp targets, filter-bar/sort overlay budget) became final UC-DISC-07, kept distinct because it is the human-gate visual/ergonomic acceptance layer (gloved-hand touch targets, 60% map visibility, dark-map pin contrast) not asserted by the functional wiring UCs.
- KEPT DISTINCT: Designer's UC-DISC-06 (empty + loading states legible and non-misleading: 300ms debounce, bbox-empty vs filter-empty messaging, Clear-filter CTA) became final UC-DISC-08. Distinct from RN's DISC-05 which only requires the overlays appear; DISC-08 owns the message-correctness and debounce behavior.
- DTL group (designer UC-DTL-01..04) carried over as canonical DTL-01..04: DTL-01 lean layout renders from real data, DTL-02 score-bar visualization primitive, DTL-03 geometry graceful degradation/centroid fallback, DTL-04 detail actions (Save + Ride It present and reachable). No internal DTL conflicts.
- OVERLAP RESOLVED (DTL-04 vs SAVE-01/SAVE-02): Designer's UC-DTL-04 asserts the Save and Ride-It buttons are PRESENT, reachable without scrolling, and show correct UX states on the detail screen; RN's UC-SAVE-01/02 own the actual persistence (curatedRouteRef write + recordRouteFeedback) and the maps deep-link mechanics. Kept distinct: DTL-04 = detail-screen affordance/UX-state acceptance; SAVE-01 = persistence-and-reopen; SAVE-02 = deep-link handoff. DTL-04's ACs reference the SAVE UCs for the underlying behavior.
- SAVE group: RN's UC-SAVE-01 (save via curatedRouteRef, appears in Saved, reopens) became final UC-SAVE-01; RN's UC-SAVE-02 (Ride-it maps deep-link util) became final UC-SAVE-02. Both renumbered within SAVE; no merge needed since they address distinct seams (persistence vs OS handoff).
