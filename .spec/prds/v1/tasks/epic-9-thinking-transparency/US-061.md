## SPECIFICATION

**Objective:** Add a mini-map preview to route attachment cards by integrating the `RouteMiniMap` component. When a route is completed, each route option card shows a small map with the polyline, making it visually clear what the route looks like before tapping.

**Success looks like:** Completed route cards in the chat show a 120pt map preview at the top of the card with the route polyline. Tapping the card selects the route and navigates to the full map view. Cards without geometry data render as before (no regression).
