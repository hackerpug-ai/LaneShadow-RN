# Archived Strategy Documents (v2.x era)

**Archived**: 2026-04-12
**Superseded by**: `.spec/PRODUCT-STRATEGY.md` (v3.0 — "Ride the Moment")

These documents represent the v2.x product strategy which framed LaneShadow as a venture-style subscription SaaS competing with Calimoto/Scenic on routing features. That direction has been retired.

**Do not use these documents for current product decisions.** They are preserved for historical reference only.

## Why Archived

The v2.x strategy optimized for subscription SaaS metrics (LTV:CAC, conversion funnels, TAM/SAM/SOM) when the founder's actual goals are: personal riding experiences, community building, sustainability as a side project, and passive revenue. The v3.0 strategy reframes LaneShadow around "ride the moment" — helping riders find the best experiences — with a community-first, lifestyle-appropriate business model.
